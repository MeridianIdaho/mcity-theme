Parks and Recreation

View Activity Guide

The Parks and Recreation Department’s mission is to enhance the community’s quality of life by providing innovatively designed parks, connected pathways, and diverse recreational opportunities for all citizens of Meridian that create lasting memories.

The department maintains roughly 255 acres of developed park land and over 100 acres of undeveloped park land, including nearly 15 miles of multi-use pathways and 4,000 trees in the urban forest system. They play host to 10 major community events such as CableONE Movie Nights, Christmas in Meridian and Unplug and Be Outside Week. The department is also in charge of community activities, sports leagues and youth camps that grow in popularity each year!

If you wish to RESERVE A PICNIC SHELTER in any of our City parks, you must do so by calling us at (208) 888-3579 or by visiting the department’s office in person on the 2nd floor of Meridian City Hall, located at 33 E. Broadway Avenue in downtown Meridian. Payment via credit card or check is required at the time of reservation. Tentative reservations or "holds" cannot be made. Welcome

Mayor's Office

Meridian is a vibrant community and a premier city to live, work, and raise a family. As Mayor my main commitment is to our fellow neighbors and friends to help them realize this vision.

Here you will find useful and timely information regarding service programs, ways to get involved in our wonderful community, as well as contact information for the Mayor's Office.

As a community our focus on being a Star of the New West begins with transforming Meridian into a 21st century city. Meridian is uniquely conducive to supporting a broad array of business activities while maintaining the high quality lifestyle and focus on families we so greatly value. Fulfilling the needs of businesses and families begins with having a safe and caring community that provides quality education, family-wage jobs, cutting-edge healthcare, diverse housing options, a trained workforce, as well as an abundance of recreation, shopping, dining, and entertainment opportunities.

If you are a lifelong resident or first-time visitor, I encourage you to explore our website and learn more about our amazing community. Consider how you can be a part of our vision and achieve your goals in Meridian.

Please feel free to stop by to contact me or my office staff regarding any questions or concerns. Communicating is easy; connect with me on Facebook or LinkedIn or visit my office located on the third floor of Meridian City Hall. Visitor parking is available in the large parking lot in front of City Hall.
